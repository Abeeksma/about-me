I was born in the cold and quiet north of Wisconsin. After graduating high school in 2009, I trained in Wildfire fighting with a close friend, both of us attempting to find a direction. About a year later I left the forest to travel and work in new places. After living in multiple cities, over roughly 6 years, I returned to study at a local Tech college. I obtained serveral tech diplomas in various welding techniques and fabrication, along with starting a technical degree in Machining. Wearing thin from the work as a contractor for the past years I decided upon a shift in career leading me here, to Delta V, where I hope to gain the neccessary skills to pursue a new and intruiging path forward.
