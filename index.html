A bit about me

Alexander Beeksma

I was born in the cold and quiet north of Wisconsin. About a year after graduating high school in 2009, I left the forest to travel and work in new places. After living in multiple cities, over roughly 6 years, I returned to study at a local Tech college. I obtained serveral tech diplomas in various welding techniques and fabrication, along with starting a technical degree in Machining. Worn from the taxing labor of construction, I decided upon a shift in career leading me here, to Delta V, where I hope to gain the neccessary skills to pursue a new and intruiging path.
